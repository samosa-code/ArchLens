# Source

A broad, deliberately diverse corpus of real, unmodified CloudFormation
templates fetched to stress-test the parser and graph pipeline (Sprints 1-2)
against far more service types, authoring styles, and template sizes than
examples 01-13 cover individually. Each file is independent (no cross-stack
relationships between them, unlike 03/06/12) -- the goal here is breadth of
real-world resource-type/intrinsic coverage, not a curated teaching example.
